About Me

As a Geospatial Data Science graduate from the University of Washington, I specialize in leveraging spatial data insights across diverse industries. My expertise lies in combining advanced GIS techniques with data science methodologies to solve complex spatial problems and drive data-informed decision-making.

My academic career at the University of Washington provided hands-on experience with various geospatial technologies and real world datasets. I developed proficiency in utilizing various GIS software and data-driven programming languages, and exploring the intersection between the two. My coursework consisted of GIS applications, interactive web mapping, statistics, data programming, and database management. This has equipped me with the skills to transform spatial data into insights and innovative solutions.
